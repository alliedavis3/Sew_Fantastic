WELCOME

to Sew Fantastic!

Creating is an addiction. When you first attempt to create something, and see tangible progress, it simply compels you forward. It’s not just the hopefully sincere comments from your friends “oh that’s so cool” or “how did you DO that?”. It’s truly the realization that you took raw materials and combined them in such a way that their cumulative value is increased dramatically. No one forgets this high. My love affair with crafting/re-purposing is the prime example of obsession. I am excited to share this obsession with you and hopefully inspire an obsession of your own. This site will focus primarily on sewing related projects but there is certainly no limit to the amount of things you can create!
